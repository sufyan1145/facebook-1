Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: July 19, 2026

You can request deletion of your Sufyan PostFire account and all associated data at any time, using either method below.

Option 1: Disconnect access yourself, immediately

  • In the Sufyan PostFire Settings page, disconnect your Google Drive and Facebook Page connections. This immediately revokes our access tokens for your Google and Facebook accounts.
  • You can also revoke access directly from your own Google Account permissions page or Facebook's "Business Integrations" settings at any time — this instantly stops Sufyan PostFire from accessing your data, independent of any action on our end.

Option 2: Request full account and data deletion

To permanently delete your Sufyan PostFire account, including your profile, schedules, upload history, activity logs, and stored access tokens, contact us directly with the email address associated with your account. We will delete your data within a reasonable time after verifying your request.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email, password hash)
  • Stored Google and Facebook access tokens
  • Schedules, upload history, and activity logs tied to your account

Video files themselves are never stored permanently by the Service (they are downloaded temporarily to process an upload and deleted immediately after), so there is no separate video archive to remove.

Contact

For data deletion requests or questions, please contact us directly.
