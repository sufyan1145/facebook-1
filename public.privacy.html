Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Drive2Facebook ("the Service", "we", "us") lets a user automatically publish video files from their own Google Drive to their own Facebook Page(s) on a schedule they control. This policy explains what data we access, why, and how it is stored and protected.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: the name, email address, and password (stored as a salted hash, never in plain text) you provide, or use to log in with.
  • Google Drive data: with your explicit authorization, we access the folders and video files you select in order to list them and upload them to Facebook. We request read-only access to your Drive — we cannot edit, delete, or share your files, and we do not access files outside the folders you connect.
  • Facebook Page data: with your explicit authorization, we access the list of Facebook Pages you manage, and we publish video posts to the specific Page(s) and at the times you configure. We do not read your personal Facebook profile, timeline, messages, or friends list.
  • Usage data: we keep logs of actions taken in your account (e.g. "schedule created", "video uploaded", "login") so you can review your own activity history inside the app.

2. How we use your information

  • To operate the core function of the Service: moving a video from your Drive folder to your Facebook Page on your schedule.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To send you account-related email (e.g. password reset) when needed.
  • To show you your own upload history, queue status, and activity logs inside the dashboard.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your Drive or Facebook data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the Service to you.

3. Data storage and retention

Account data and activity logs are stored in a private database. OAuth access tokens for Google and Facebook are stored encrypted and are only used to make the API calls needed to run your schedules. Video files are downloaded temporarily to process an upload and are deleted immediately afterward — we do not keep a permanent copy of your video files on our servers.

4. Sharing of information

We do not share your personal data, Drive contents, or Facebook data with third parties, except:

  • With Google and Meta (Facebook), solely to perform the actions you've authorized (reading your Drive files, publishing to your Page).
  • Where required by law.

5. Your choices and control

  • You can disconnect your Google Drive or Facebook Page connection at any time from Settings, which revokes our access.
  • You can delete individual schedules or your entire account by contacting us.
  • Revoking access via your Google Account or Facebook App settings immediately stops the Service from accessing that account.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed, sessions use signed authentication tokens, and third-party access tokens are encrypted at rest. Access to the Service requires an account created for you — the Service is invite-only and not open to public self-signup.

7. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at children and is not knowingly used by anyone under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

9. Contact

For any privacy questions, deletion requests, or to request an account, please contact us directly.
